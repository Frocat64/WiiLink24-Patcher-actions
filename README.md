## WiiLink24 Patcher

WiiLink24 Patcher is a Homebrew application made for easier installation of WiiLink24. With it, you can just sit back and relax while the patcher does everything for you.

### Features
* No PC required - update and patch channels directly on your console!
* The patcher will automatically download any files that are required to patch everything.
* Updates are as simple as patching the channel again!

>This patcher may contain bugs. If you spot any or are just having problems with the patcher in general, report them on our [Issues Page](https://github.com/WiiLink24/WiiLink24-Patcher/issues), or ask us for help on our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/n4ta3w6)!

>Looking for the previous, PC-based patcher? Check out the [pc-patcher](https://github.com/WiiLink24/WiiLink24-Patcher/tree/pc-patcher) branch!
